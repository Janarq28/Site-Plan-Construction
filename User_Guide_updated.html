Centralized visibility
across all properties

The CP Site Plan Construction Dashboard consolidates legal, construction, and contract information for all lots and condominiums in the Costa Palmas Master Plan — 1,414 total records.

01 — Descripción General

Visibilidad centralizada
de todas las propiedades

El Dashboard CP Site Plan Construction consolida la información legal, de construcción y de contratos de todos los lotes y condominios del Master Plan de Costa Palmas — 1,414 registros en total.

Centralized Information

Legal, construction, and contract data unified in a single interactive view — no manual lookups across systems.

Interactive Site Plan

Navigate the Costa Palmas master plan map. Click any orange pin to filter all dashboard pages to that neighborhood instantly.

Construction Visibility

Track milestones from construction start through substantial completion and occupancy delivery date.

Contract Monitoring

Monitor the full contract lifecycle — list date, effective date, firm date, and closed status — for every unit.

ℹDesigned for: Executives & project leads · Sales & legal teams · Construction managers · CPH Operations Team staff

03 — Páginas del Dashboard

Páginas de
navegación

Site Plan Master

Main navigation map. Allows users to identify neighborhoods and select a location to filter the dashboard.

04 — Glossary

Construction
date definitions

Reference definitions for the key construction and delivery milestones shown in the dashboard.

04 — Glosario

Definiciones de
fechas de construcción

Definiciones de referencia para los principales hitos de construcción y entrega mostrados en el dashboard.

TermDefinition
Construction Start DateDate when construction activities officially begin for the property.
Substantial CompletionDate when the property is expected to reach a stage where it can be used for its intended purpose, although minor outstanding items may remain.
Substantial Completion NoticeFormal notification issued to the owner indicating that the property has achieved Substantial Completion and is ready for its intended use, subject to resolution of minor outstanding items, also known as Punch List.
Punch ListList of minor corrective items, adjustments, or deficiencies identified after Substantial Completion that must be completed before Final Completion.
Final CompletionDate when all Punch List items have been fully completed and resolved.
Occupancy DateDate when the owner is authorized to occupy the property after Final Completion and after all documentation, payments, insurance requirements, and related obligations have been satisfied.
Project CloseoutFinal project milestone indicating that all contractual and financial obligations with the owner have been fulfilled. In most projects, this occurs at the end of the warranty period, typically approximately one year after Substantial Completion.

Substantial CompletionFecha en la que se espera que la propiedad alcance una etapa en la que pueda utilizarse para su propósito previsto, aunque puedan existir pendientes menores.
Substantial Completion NoticeNotificación formal emitida al propietario indicando que la propiedad ha alcanzado Substantial Completion y está lista para su uso previsto, sujeta a la resolución de pendientes menores, también conocidos como Punch List.

Project CloseoutHito final del proyecto que indica que todas las obligaciones contractuales y financieras con el propietario han sido cumplidas. En la mayoría de los proyectos, ocurre al finalizar el periodo de garantía, normalmente aproximadamente un año después del Substantial Completion.

06 — How to Interact

Navigation &
cross-filtering

Every element in the dashboard is interactive. Clicking visuals, rows, or pins applies filters that propagate across all other elements on the page.

06 — Cómo Interactuar

Navegación y
filtrado cruzado

Cada elemento del dashboard es interactivo. Hacer clic en visualizaciones, filas o pins aplica filtros que se propagan a todos los demás elementos en la página.

⊙

Hover for tooltips

Hover over any KPI card, table row, or map element to reveal a detailed tooltip with additional data.

⇄

Cross-filtering

Selecting any element — table row, bar, or pin — automatically filters all other visuals on that page.

◉

Map pins

Clicking a neighborhood pin on the Site Plan page instantly filters every other section to that area.

⊞

Slicer behavior

Top-of-page slicers apply globally. Use CTRL+Click for multi-select. Click a selected value again to deselect.

↗

Current Selection bar

The bar at the bottom of every page shows active filters. Always validate before any export or analysis.

✕

Double-click to clear

Double-click any visual element to clear its cross-filter selection and reset that visual to all data.

08 — Best Practices

Get the most
from the dashboard

Five recommendations for a consistent, reliable experience.

08 — Mejores Prácticas

Aprovecha al máximo
el dashboard

Cinco recomendaciones para una experiencia consistente y confiable.

01

Use a desktop browser

Access on a desktop or laptop at full resolution. Mobile viewports compress the layout and reduce readability of the data tables.

02

Enable full-screen mode

Press F11 or use Power BI's built-in full-screen button to maximize the visual area and eliminate browser chrome.

03

Validate Current Selection before exporting

Check the bar at the bottom of each page to confirm which filters are active. This prevents exporting incomplete or unintended data slices.

04

Use Property Code for precise lookups

The Property Code filter is the fastest and most precise way to locate a specific unit across all five dashboard pages simultaneously.

05

Refresh the browser if visuals fail to load

If charts or maps appear blank or frozen, a browser refresh re-establishes the data connection and resolves most rendering issues.

01

Usa un navegador de escritorio

Accede desde una computadora de escritorio o laptop a resolución completa. Las pantallas móviles comprimen el diseño y reducen la legibilidad de las tablas.

02

Activa el modo pantalla completa

Presiona F11 o usa el botón de pantalla completa de Power BI para maximizar el área visual.

03

Valida la Selección Actual antes de exportar

Verifica la barra en la parte inferior de cada página para confirmar qué filtros están activos. Esto evita exportar datos incompletos o no deseados.

04

Usa el Código de Propiedad para búsquedas precisas

El filtro de Código de Propiedad es la forma más rápida y precisa de localizar una unidad específica en las cinco páginas del dashboard.

05

Recarga el navegador si los visuales no cargan

Si las gráficas o mapas aparecen en blanco o congelados, recargar el navegador restablece la conexión de datos y resuelve la mayoría de los problemas.
